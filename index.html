About

Welcome to our cozy taco shop, serving Ocean Beach and Point Loma since 2004. We're all about simple, friendly service and delicious, fresh Mexican food.

Our signature California burrito and Sabroso (surf and turf) are local favorites, and our tacos - like Carnitas and Asada - are a hit with customers. Come on in, try us out, and taste the difference for yourself. We're confident you'll leave feeling full, happy, and eager to return.
